Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how onesearch ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

onesearch is designed with a minimal data footprint. We do not collect browsing behaviour, sell data, or share your information with third parties. The only personal data we store is an email address you voluntarily provide to use the post-liking feature.

Information We Collect

We collect and store only the following:

  • Email address — collected when you choose to like a post. Stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and on our servers solely to record your like. We do not use this email for marketing or share it with third parties.

We do not collect names, passwords, IP addresses, device identifiers, or browsing history.

Jira Integration

If you choose to connect your Atlassian Jira account, onesearch uses OAuth 2.0 to request read-only access. The following applies:

  • Your Jira issues are fetched directly from Atlassian to your browser — they never pass through or are stored on our servers
  • We request only the read:jira-work and read:me scopes — no write, delete, or admin permissions
  • An OAuth access token is stored in your server-side session for the duration of your browser session and is cleared on disconnect or browser close
  • We never receive or store your Atlassian password
  • Your Atlassian account ID is not stored in our database

Connecting Jira is entirely optional. The app is fully functional without it. Before connecting, please ensure your use is permitted under your employer's IT and acceptable-use policies.

Cookies & Sessions

onesearch uses a server-side session cookie solely to maintain your Jira OAuth token during an active browser session. This cookie:

  • Contains no personally identifiable information directly
  • Is invalidated when you disconnect Jira or close the browser
  • Is not used for tracking or advertising

No third-party cookies or tracking scripts are used on this site.

Data Storage & Retention

Email addresses provided for the liking feature are stored in our database and associated with the specific posts you liked. We do not store any Jira or Atlassian workspace content.

To request deletion of your email and associated likes, contact us at the address below.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third parties. onesearch does not integrate with advertising networks, analytics platforms, or data-brokering services.

Security

All communication between onesearch and Atlassian services is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Our servers are hosted on Microsoft Azure with access controls in place. No security system is infallible — in the event of a breach we will notify affected users promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
