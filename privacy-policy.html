Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how onesearch ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you install and use our Atlassian application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

✓  onesearch does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All data access is temporary, used only to serve your in-session requests.

Information We Access

To provide its functionality, onesearch may temporarily access:

  • Atlassian project and issue data within your authorized workspace
  • Your Atlassian account identifier (AccountID) for authentication purposes
  • Search queries you submit within the app

This data is accessed in real-time to process your requests and is never stored, logged, or retained beyond your active session.

Data Storage

onesearch does not store personal data in any database or external system. We do not cache user data for more than the duration of a single request. No Atlassian AccountIDs, names, email addresses, or workspace content are persisted on our servers.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share your information with any third parties. onesearch operates exclusively within the Atlassian ecosystem and does not integrate with external advertising, analytics, or data-brokering services.

Atlassian Marketplace Compliance

onesearch is built in accordance with the Atlassian Marketplace Privacy Policy requirements . We request only the following OAuth scopes, and nothing more:

  • read:jira-work — Read project and issue data from Jira
  • read:me — Read your basic Atlassian account profile (name, account ID)

No write, admin, or any other scopes are requested or used.

Security

All communication between onesearch and Atlassian services is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Authentication is handled entirely through Atlassian's OAuth 2.0 framework — we never receive or store your Atlassian password.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
